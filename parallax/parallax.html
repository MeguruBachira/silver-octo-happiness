Here's the story of Lucas and Abigial

Prologue

There was a dectective named Lucas, he was renowned as the worlds greatest detective. He was born March 12th, 1944.

As a child he showed he was very intelligent, so much so he would help the police solve a murder case at the age of 13,

While attening school he would help the police for regualrly, from burglary to arson Lucas would solve every case he was assigned to help with

After finishing school the chief of the police would ask,

"Would you like to work with us?"

Lucas would accept gratefully and would take the position of detective and over the next 10 years he would solve any and every case he was assigned to.

But even the best start to falter at some point

Once Lucas was in his late 30s his 100 percent case solve rate had plummted alot, having 200 cases he said were

"Dead Ends"

Soon the chief made a suggestion, he one day said to Lucas

"You should take in an apprentice, that why when you retire you have someone to take over your legacy"

At first Lucas was against this idea but eventually he caved and got an apprentice

Chapter 1, Abigial

One day the chief brought in a 13 year old girl and introduced her to Lucas

"Her name is Abigail and she will be your new apprentice" He said with a smile.

Abigail was a quite girl who didn't say much but was very good at solving crimes, like Lucas she solved a murder case at 13

They also had similarities in other things like the way they would eat and drink

The chief would often joke and say

"She's pretty much a clone of you" with nice smile

Lucas would often laugh aswell and say

"She's good but she can't replace the best"

Abigail first found the joke funny but overtime she realized that Lucas was belittling her

This would drive her to do better then Lucas

"But what can I do to prove I'm better the Lucas?"

Then it hit her

The pile of over 200 cases that Lucas couldn't solve

"If I can solve all of them I'll become the next Lucas!"

So she got to work

Chapter 2, Evidence

Abigail has been secretly working on all the cases that Lucas couldn't solve for about 2 years.

She took a step back to look at the crime board, she fell to her knees

"No way, it can't be" she told herself

What kind of revelation would make Abigail fall to her knees, you ask?

"The person behind all of this, the reason these crimes are unsolved is because Lucas was intertwined with all of them"

But how can she prove that Lucas was the mastermind behind all this she needed a solid undeniable piece of evidence

So she reviewed everything and found a date March 12

"That's the day the next crime he will commit will happen" she said, so she began planning

Chapter 2.5, Evidence

So March 12 came around and Abigail began trailing Lucas

He did nothing out of the ordinary, went shopping, bought food, and was hanging out with family and friends

"Damn it," Abigail thought "Was I wrong about Lucas committing all the crimes"

Just as she thought it was all over she saw Lucas looking around himself and scanning the surronding area

Then he did it, he walked into an abandoned building with boarded up windows

"I was correct" Abigail thought but she had to be sure

She got on top of the building and noticed a open window

"I can get in there" she thought so she carefully climbed down and entered the window

Nothing could have prepared her for what she was going to see.

Chapter 3, Solved

Abigail had found more then enough evidence to prove it was Lucas at committed the crimes.

Drug routes, tons of cash, dead bodies hidden in boxes, and plans for future crimes.

Abigail took what she needed and left, absolutely mortified by what she had seen

The following day she came to Lucas's desk with a box

"Good morning Abigail, what's the box for?" he asked

"Oh it's your birthday gift but I need to set it up, you don't mind do you"

"Not at all go ahead I love suprises" he said with a laugh afterwards

"Do you mind if I blindfold you and also can I use your handcuffs?" Abigail said setting her plan into motion

"Sure I'm excitied about what will happen now" Lucas said

"Got him" Abigail thought

She quickly blindfolded Lucas and then handcuffed him to his chair

Abigail started laying out the contents in the box.

It was evidence from the house she was at last night,

"Alright let me get the chief so the suprise is complete" Abigail said running away

"Wait why do you need the chief, Abigail, ABIGAIL" Lucas exclaimed

Abigail explained what happened last night to the chief who was doubtful at first but as he listened and walked with her, things started to make sense

At last the arrived at Lucas's desk

"Here's the criminal and the evidence to send him to prison" Abigail said and walked out

"I cant believe I looked up to a person like that" were her final words to the chief and Lucas before she left

The chief had to tell everyone that Lucas was a criminal and of course everyone thought it was some joke but sadly it was the truth

Abigail would be renowned as a hero for catching the mastermind behind all of those crimes and would be the new head dectective

And Abigail would go to make a quote that she would repeat constantly

"Even the kindest and smartest people can be criminals" - Abigail 1983
